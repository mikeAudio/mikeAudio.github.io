Get your record at:

Lighthouse Studio

 

Whether it's the first single or the tenth studio album:

At Lighthouse Studio, we use our many years of experience to show your music at its best. As a professional DIY studio, we combine good quality and passionate work with favourable prices and a musical collaboration at eye level. We offer all the important services in a cosy atmosphere and deliver a product that only needs to be uploaded to Spotify.



Recording

In our acoustically calibrated recording room, we have space for up to 8 musicians, as well as a spacious, well-shielded booth for anything related to the drums. With our generous selection of microphones, we can find the right sound for you.

Mix & Master

From singer/songwriters, punk and black metal to jazz, folk and hip-hop: we have mixing and mastering experience in various genres. We are also happy to mix and master projects that were not recorded in our studio.

Pricing

Every production is individual and every musician has their own requirements. As we aim to encourage young people in particular to embark on the path to their first recording, we always adapt to the financial possibilities of our customers.

On a balcony on a sunny spring morning in Kiel: in an aside, someone says: "Well, all what's missing now is our own recording studio". What began as a loose idea quickly became a reality. An application for funding, many litres of coffee and almost a year later, the Lighthouse Studio opened its doors.


With the aim of making music recording possible for beginners, professionals and everyone in between, we decided to combine our many years of experience as musicians and producers with a DIY approach. A spacious, self-built separate drum booth, complemented by excellent DIY absorbers create a pleasant atmosphere in our recording room, both for the eyes and the ears. Partly self-built or second-hand furniture, decoration and many other creative ideas make the Lighthouse Studio a cosy place for professional music recordings. Whether solo recordings or live sessions - up to 8 musicians have the opportunity to let off steam musically on 90 square metres.




Micha Kropf

After 27 years in Hamburg, I decided to move to Kiel in 2022. I've been making music for about 20 years, playing guitar, bass and singing in my main band "Waldgeist Kartell". Due to my experience in songwriting and my extensive collaboration with other musicians, I am mainly responsible for recording at Lighthouse Studio. In recent years I have produced music with various artists (including La Loba, Sebastian der Saubere & Colin Robert Dexter) and also gained several years of stage experience with various band and solo projects. In addition to the studio, I work as a night leader in the Kiel concert house and nightclub "Schaubude".
